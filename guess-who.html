How to play

  • 📱Open this page on both devices and pick the same set.
  • 🤫Each player gets a secret character. Tap the eye to see yours.
  • 💬Take turns asking yes or no questions out loud in the language you're learning.
  • 🚫Tap a card to grey it out when it can't be the answer.
  • 🏆First to guess the other player's character wins.
Tip: switch your label language anytime with the ع / NL button.
